Frequently Asked Questions

How do I delete my data?

Go to Settings > Clear All Data. This will permanently remove all your thoughts, progress, and settings.

The app won't open. What should I do?

  • Try restarting your device
  • Make sure you have the latest iOS version
  • Delete and reinstall the app
  • If the issue persists, contact us at the email above

Does Mental OS collect my data?

No. All your data stays on your device. We don't collect, store, or transmit any personal information. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Can I export my data?

Yes! Go to Settings > Export Data. You can export your thoughts and progress in various formats.

How do I suggest a feature?

We'd love to hear from you! Email us at mark@huthmaker.com with your ideas.

Is this a replacement for therapy?

No. Mental OS is a wellness tool, not a substitute for professional mental health care. If you're experiencing a mental health crisis, please contact:

  • 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline: Call or text 988
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
  • Or contact a qualified mental health professional
